Job Search Guide

A simple, repeatable approach to find the right role faster.

1) Targeted search

  • Identify 2–3 job titles and 3–5 target companies.
  • Set alerts and check the portal regularly for new postings.

2) Tailor each application

  • Customize your resume summary and one or two bullets per job.
  • Include keywords from the job description.

3) Network proactively

  • Reach out to people at target companies with a short, courteous message.
  • Use LinkedIn introductions and follow-up after applying.

4) Follow up & track

  • Send a brief follow-up 5–7 days after applying if you haven't heard back.
  • Keep a simple spreadsheet of applications, dates, and next steps.
