Interview Prep

Practical steps to prepare and perform confidently.

Before the interview

  • Research the company and the role; read the job description carefully.
  • Prepare 4–6 STAR stories (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
  • Practice common technical / role-specific questions aloud.

During the interview

  • Listen carefully — pause before answering to structure your response.
  • If you don't know an answer, explain how you'd approach it.
  • Ask thoughtful questions about the team, expectations and next steps.

Technical interviews

  • Talk through your thinking; the interviewer values your approach.
  • Write clean, testable code and run simple examples if possible.

Day-of checklist

  1. Confirm time and meeting link / location.
  2. Charge your devices, find a quiet spot, test audio/video.
  3. Dress appropriately and be on time (5–10 minutes early).
